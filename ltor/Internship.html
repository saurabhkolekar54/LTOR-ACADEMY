Unlock Your Potential with Our Internship Program

About Internship

Explore real-world projects under the mentorship of Kale sir, our esteemed Corporate Trainer. The internship includes a technical interview, HR interview, and a final meeting with the technical management team for selected candidates. A minimum 3-month commitment is required, and successful interns receive an Internship Certificate.

About Stipend

Stipends are awarded to candidates handling clients independently. Choose from technologies like Full Stack Development, App Development, Digital Marketing, Data Science, HR Management, and Entrepreneur Skill Development. Opportunities for recruitment may follow a successful internship.

Internship Requirements

  • Submit 2 photos, Aadhar card Xerox, Last Educational Xerox certificate, and 2 copies of the resume.
  • Compulsory attendance on every working day.
  • Daily schedule includes 2 hours of training and 4 hours of project work.
  • Sundays are holidays.

Daily Work Schedule

  • Compulsory attendance every working day.
  • 2 hours of training and 4 hours of project work daily.
  • Sunday is the only holiday.

Additional Information

  • Mandatory preparation and reporting to the senior team.
  • Progress reports by HR are required.
  • Award ceremonies are held every Friday.
  • Upon joining, candidates receive the required syllabus Excel file for the chosen technology.
