About Us

Welcome to LTOR Education, a leading professional training institute in Pune since 2011. We are dedicated to providing high-quality education and skill development opportunities to our students.

At LTOR, we offer a range of courses, including Spoken English, Public Speaking, Study Skills, Business Skill Development, and Soft Skills Training. Our experienced instructors and comprehensive curriculum ensure that our students acquire the knowledge and skills needed to excel in their chosen fields.

Our commitment extends beyond the classroom. We actively engage in various business activities, such as training, placement, marketing, business meet-ups, exhibitions, and seminars. Additionally, we offer franchise opportunities for those interested in partnering with us to spread quality education.

Explore our website to learn more about our courses, events, volunteer opportunities, and how LTOR can contribute to your personal and professional growth.
